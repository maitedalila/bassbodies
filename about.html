Our Story

Founders

Labor day weekend, a few years ago, two sisters got in their cars without any plans and drove 9 hours to New York City to experience their first music festival. Memories would be created, DJs would be met, and an idea for a new workout program would be born. Fast forward to today and Gabriella and Giuliana Leone have taken their idea of a "rave-like workout" to the next level through Bass Bodies.

Bass Bodies is all about burning to the beat of electronic dance music through a variety of workout programs and dance in a rave like atmosphere. The class takes everything you love about music festivals, the music, the fashion, the kandi, the good vibes, and has combined them into a workout. Bass Bodies is not just a workout, it's a party that is easy to follow, and everyone is invited to burn to the beat with us!
