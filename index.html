Bass Bodies Electronic Fitness ™ fuses electronic dance music with fitness and dancing to create a high calorie blasting workout. We burn, box, build, and breath to the beat to keep your body moving to the rhythm of the music and your mind motivated to crush your workout.

Workouts

Burn to the Beat

High intensity interval training fused with electronic dance moves, working the whole body. Calorie burn on average is 1000+ Class sometimes includes weights for intervals depending.

Box to the Beat

Cardio kickboxing class working the whole body and promoting strength. Calorie burn on average is 1100+ and sometimes includes light hand weights.

Build to the Beat

Light weight lifting class plus floor exercises for abs and booty toning - the focus here is not on calorie burn, however it is around 500-800 depending on weights used and amount of reps.
